Achutam Keshavam

अच्युतम् केशवम्

Full Bhajan Lyrics in Hindi with Meaning

📚 Bhajan 🪤 Lord Vishnu 🌐 Hindi + English

Achutam Keshavam (अच्युतम् केशवम्) is one of the most melodious and beloved devotional bhajans dedicated to Lord Vishnu and Krishna. 'Achyuta' means the infallible, imperishable one; 'Keshava' is another name of Lord Krishna. This bhajan, popularized by various spiritual artists, describes the divine qualities of Lord Vishnu and brings immense peace.

Achutam Keshavam — अच्युतम् केशवम्

अच्युतम् केशवम् कृष्ण दामोदरम् राम नारायणम् जानकी वल्लभम्। कौस्तुभ मोहनम् शंख चक्रधरम् वासुदेवम् हरे तारकं ब्रह्म हि॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

अच्युतम् केशवम् अच्युतम् केशवम् कृष्ण दामोदरम् राम वासुदेवम्। अच्युतम् केशवम् अच्युतम् केशवम् जय जय देवकी नंदनम् गोविंदम्॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

जय जय राम कृष्ण हरि। जय जय राम कृष्ण हरि॥ नारायण नारायण नारायण जय। नारायण नारायण नारायण जय॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

शांताकारम् भुजगशयनम् पद्मनाभं सुरेशम्। विश्वाधारम् गगनसदृशम् मेघवर्णम् शुभाङ्गम्॥ लक्ष्मीकान्तम् कमलनयनम् योगिभिर्ध्यानगम्यम्। वन्दे विष्णुं भवभयहरम् सर्वलोकैकनाथम्॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

Achyutam Keshavam Krishna Damodaram Rama Narayanam Janaki Vallabham. Kaustubha Mohanam Shankha Chakradharam Vasudevam Hare Tarakam Brahma Hi.

Shantakaram Bhujagas-hayanam Padma-nabham Suresham Vishwa-dharam Gagana-sadrisham Megha-varnam Shubhangam. Lakshmi-kantam Kamala-nayanam Yogi-bhir-dhyana-gamyam Vande Vishnum Bhava-bhaya-haram Sarva-lokaikantham.

🌸 Significance
Singing or listening to Achutam Keshavam fills the heart with divine love and devotion. It is a perfect bhajan for morning prayers, meditation sessions, and temple worship.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does Achutam Keshavam mean?

Achyutam means 'the infallible, imperishable one' — a name of Lord Vishnu. Keshavam means 'one with beautiful hair' — a name of Lord Krishna. Together they invoke the Supreme Lord Vishnu-Krishna.

Who composed Achutam Keshavam?

Achutam Keshavam is a traditional Vaishnava bhajan combining Sanskrit shlokas praising Lord Vishnu. The 'Shantakaram' verse is from the ancient Vishnu Stuti found in the Puranas.

What is the best time to sing this bhajan?

Achutam Keshavam is perfect for morning prayers (Brahma Muhurta), during Vishnu puja, on Ekadashi, on Thursdays (Guruvar), and on special Vaishnava occasions like Janmashtami.
