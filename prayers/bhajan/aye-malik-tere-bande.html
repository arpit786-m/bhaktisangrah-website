Aye Malik Tere Bande Hum

ऐ मालिक तेरे बंदे हम

Soulful Prayer for Guidance and Forgiveness

📚 Bhajan 🪤 God (Paramatma) 🌐 Hindi + English

'Aye Malik Tere Bande Hum' (ऐ मालिक तेरे बंदे हम) is a deeply moving prayer song from the classic 1957 film Do Aankhen Barah Haath. Sung by Lata Mangeshkar and V. Shantaram, and written by Bharat Vyas, it is a universal plea to the Almighty for guidance out of darkness and forgiveness for worldly sins.

Aye Malik Tere Bande Hum — ऐ मालिक तेरे बंदे हम

ऐ मालिक तेरे बंदे हम। ऐसे हों हमारे करम॥ नेकी पर चलें, और बदी से टलें। ताकि हँसते हुए निकले दम॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

ये अँधेरा घना छा रहा। तेरा इंसान घबरा रहा॥ हो रहा बेखबर, कुछ ना आता नज़र। सुख का सूरज छिपा जा रहा॥ है तेरी रोशनी में वो दम। जो अमावस को कर दे पूनम॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

बड़ा कमज़ोर है आदमी। अभी लाखों हैं इसमें कमी॥ पर तू जो खड़ा, है दयालू बड़ा। तेरी कृपा से धरती थमी॥ दिया तूने हमें जब जनम। तू ही झेलेगा हम सबके ग़म॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

जब ज़ुल्मों का हो सामना। तब तू ही हमें थामना॥ वो बुराई करें, हम भलाई भरें। नहीं बदले की हो कामना॥ बढ़ उठे प्यार का हर कदम। और मिटे बैर का ये भरम॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

Ai Maalik Tere Bnde Hama. Aise Hon Hamaare Karama.. Nekee Par Chalen, Aur Badee Se Talen. Taaki Hnsate Hue Nikale Dama..

Ye Andheraa Ghanaa Chhaa Rahaa. Teraa Insaan Ghabaraa Rahaa.. Ho Rahaa Bekhabara, Kuchh Naa Aataa Najra. Sukh Kaa Sooraj Chhipaa Jaa Rahaa.. Hai Teree Roshanee Men Vo Dama. Jo Amaavas Ko Kar De Poonama..

Badaa Kamajor Hai Aadamee. Abhee Laakhon Hain Isamen Kamee.. Par Too Jo Khadaa, Hai Dayaaloo Badaa. Teree Kripaa Se Dharatee Thamee.. Diyaa Toone Hamen Jab Janama. Too Hee Jhelegaa Ham Sabake Gma..

Jab Julmon Kaa Ho Saamanaa. Tab Too Hee Hamen Thaamanaa.. Vo Buraaee Karen, Ham Bhalaaee Bharen. Naheen Badale Kee Ho Kaamanaa.. Badh Uthe Pyaar Kaa Har Kadama. Aur Mite Bair Kaa Ye Bharama..

🌸 Significance
The bhajan highlights human vulnerability and the infinite compassion of God. It asks for the strength to do good deeds despite facing hardships and evil, trusting that God's grace will ultimately prevail.

Frequently Asked Questions

From which movie is 'Aye Malik Tere Bande Hum'?

It is from the legendary 1957 Bollywood film 'Do Aankhen Barah Haath', directed by V. Shantaram. The film is known for its strong moral message about reforming prisoners.

Who sang this beautiful bhajan?

The lead vocals were provided by the legendary Lata Mangeshkar, with a chorus backing. V. Shantaram himself also features in the vocal rendition in the film.

What does 'Taaki haste hue nikle dam' mean?

It translates to 'So that when our life ends (nikle dam), we leave this world smiling (haste hue).' It is a prayer to live such a righteous life that one has no regrets at the time of death.
