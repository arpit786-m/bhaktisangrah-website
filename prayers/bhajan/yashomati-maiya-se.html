Yashomati Maiya Se Bole Nandlala

यशोमती मैया से बोले नंदलाला

Beautiful Krishna Bhajan from Satyam Shivam Sundaram

📚 Bhajan 🪤 Lord Krishna 🌐 Hindi + English

'Yashomati Maiya Se Bole Nandlala' (यशोमती मैया से बोले नंदलाला) is a profoundly sweet devotional song portraying the innocent childhood (Bal Leela) of Lord Krishna. Featuring in the 1978 film Satyam Shivam Sundaram, the song captures young Krishna asking his mother Yashoda why Radha is fair while he is dark-skinned.

Yashomati Maiya Se Bole Nandlala — यशोमती मैया से बोले नंदलाला

यशोमती मैया से बोले नंदलाला। राधा क्यों गोरी, मैं क्यों काला॥ राधा क्यों गोरी, मैं क्यों काला॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

बोली मुस्काती मैया ललन को बताया। कारी अँधियरी आधी रात में तू आया॥ लाड़ला कन्हैया मेरा हो, लाड़ला कन्हैया मेरा काली कमली वाला। राधा क्यों गोरी, मैं क्यों काला॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

गोरी गोरी राधिका के नैन कजरारे। काले काले नैनों से जादू किया रे॥ जादू किया रे॥ काले काले नैनों से जादू किया रे॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

तभी तो ये पूछे हो... तभी तो ये पूछे देवकी का लाला। राधा क्यों गोरी, मैं क्यों काला॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

Yashomatee Maiyaa Se Bole Nndalaalaa. Raadhaa Kyon Goree, Main Kyon Kaalaa.. Raadhaa Kyon Goree, Main Kyon Kaalaa..

Bolee Muskaatee Maiyaa Lalan Ko Bataayaa. Kaaree Andhiyaree Aadhee Raat Men Too Aayaa.. Laadlaa Kanhaiyaa Meraa Ho, Laadlaa Kanhaiyaa Meraa Kaalee Kamalee Vaalaa. Raadhaa Kyon Goree, Main Kyon Kaalaa..

Goree Goree Raadhikaa Ke Nain Kajaraare. Kaale Kaale Nainon Se Jaadoo Kiyaa Re.. Jaadoo Kiyaa Re.. Kaale Kaale Nainon Se Jaadoo Kiyaa Re..

Tabhee To Ye Poochhe Ho... Tabhee To Ye Poochhe Devakee Kaa Laalaa. Raadhaa Kyon Goree, Main Kyon Kaalaa..

🌸 Significance
The bhajan beautifully highlights the pure, innocent, and playful nature of the divine. Mother Yashoda's loving response — that his dark complexion is the color of the rain-bearing clouds and the deep night, masking his divine mystery — fills devotees with immense prem and vatsalya bhava (parental devotion).

Frequently Asked Questions

Who sang Yashomati Maiya Se Bole Nandlala?

The classic original was sung by Lata Mangeshkar for the 1978 Raj Kapoor film 'Satyam Shivam Sundaram'. The music was composed by Laxmikant-Pyarelal with lyrics by Pandit Narendra Sharma.

What is the meaning of the bhajan?

Little Krishna (Nandlala) innocently asks his mother Yashoda why Radha is so fair-skinned while he is dark. Mother Yashoda lovingly replies that he was born on a dark, stormy midnight (Ashtami), which is why he has the beautiful dark hue of the night sky.
