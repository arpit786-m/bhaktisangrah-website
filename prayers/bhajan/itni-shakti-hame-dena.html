Itni Shakti Hame Dena Data

इतनी शक्ति हमें देना दाता

Classic Morning School Prayer & Devotional Bhajan

📚 Bhajan 🪤 God (Paramatma) 🌐 Hindi + English

'Itni Shakti Hame Dena Data' (इतनी शक्ति हमें देना दाता) is one of the most beloved and universal morning prayers in India. Penned by Abhilash for the 1986 film Ankush, it serves as a daily reminder to choose the path of righteousness, ask for strength to overcome inner weakness, and spread peace.

Itni Shakti Hame Dena Data — इतनी शक्ति हमें देना दाता

इतनी शक्ति हमें देना दाता। मन का विश्वास कमज़ोर हो ना॥ हम चलें नेक रस्ते पे हमसे। भूलकर भी कोई भूल हो ना॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

दूर अज्ञान के हो अंधेरे। तू हमें ज्ञान की रोशनी दे॥ हर बुराई से बचकर रहें हम। जितनी भी दे भली ज़िंदगी दे॥ बैर हो ना किसी का किसी से। भावना मन में बदले की हो ना॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

हम न सोचें हमें क्या मिला है। हम ये सोचें किया क्या है अर्पण॥ फूल खुशियों के बाँटें सभी को। सबका जीवन ही बन जाए मधुबन॥ अपनी करुणा का जल तू बहा के। कर दे पावन हर इक मन का कोना॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

Itanee Shakti Hamen Denaa Daataa. Man Kaa Vishvaas Kamajor Ho Naa.. Ham Chalen Nek Raste Pe Hamase. Bhoolakar Bhee Koee Bhool Ho Naa..

Door Ajnyaan Ke Ho Andhere. Too Hamen Jnyaan Kee Roshanee De.. Har Buraaee Se Bachakar Rahen Hama. Jitanee Bhee De Bhalee Jindagee De.. Bair Ho Naa Kisee Kaa Kisee Se. Bhaavanaa Man Men Badale Kee Ho Naa..

Ham N Sochen Hamen Kyaa Milaa Hai. Ham Ye Sochen Kiyaa Kyaa Hai Arpana.. Phool Khushiyon Ke Baanten Sabhee Ko. Sabakaa Jeevan Hee Ban Jaae Madhubana.. Apanee Karunaa Kaa Jal Too Bahaa Ke. Kar De Paavan Har Ik Man Kaa Konaa..

🌸 Significance
This bhajan holds a special place in millions of hearts as a childhood school prayer. It asks the Almighty not for material wealth, but for the inner strength to stay humble, true, and away from the darkness of ignorance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who wrote Itni Shakti Hame Dena Data?

The song was written by lyricist Abhilash and composed by Kuldeep Singh for the 1986 Bollywood movie Ankush. It was sung by Pushpa Pagdhare and Sushma Shrestha.

Why is this song so popular?

Its profound yet simple message of seeking inner strength instead of material wealth, and its universal, non-denominational appeal made it the standard morning prayer in thousands of Indian schools.

What is the core message of the bhajan?

The core message is to pray for the strength to walk the path of righteousness, to avoid revenge and hatred, and to selflessly offer happiness to others (किया क्या है अर्पण).
