Sunderkand Path

सुंदरकांड पाठ

Key Verses from Valmiki Ramayana — Hanuman's Journey to Lanka

📚 Stotra 🪤 Lord Hanuman 🌐 Hindi + English

Sunderkand (सुंदरकांड) is the fifth book (kanda) of Valmiki's Ramayana, describing Lord Hanuman's extraordinary journey to Lanka to find Sita Mata. It is the most auspicious section of the Ramayana and is considered a complete prayer in itself. Reciting Sunderkand removes all obstacles, fulfills desires, and brings immense blessings of Hanuman and Ram.

Sunderkand Path — सुंदरकांड पाठ

शांत मूर्ति सुमित्रानंदन पंत की जय॥ जय हनुमान ज्ञान गुण सागर। जय कपीश तिहुं लोक उजागर॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

जामवंत के वचन सुहाए। सुनि हनुमंत हृदय हरषाए॥ तब लगि मोहि परिखेहु तुम भाई। सहि दुख कंद मूल फल खाई॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

जब हनुमंत समुद्र लांघे। सोहत मुख मानो रबि आगे॥ लंका निकट गए हनुमंत। देखेन्हि लंका अति सुंदर संत॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

बिनु गह छपन हो सो जाई। देखेउ नगर अति सुंदराई॥ सोने की लंका अति विभूषित। हनुमत मन प्रमुदित उत्साहित॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

सीता माँ को लंका में पाया। हनुमत ने उन्हें ढांढस दिलाया॥ राम की मुद्रिका दिखाई माँ को। संदेश राम का सुनाया सबको॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

अशोक वाटिका उजाड़ दीन्हा। अक्षय कुमार को मार दीन्हा॥ लंका को आग लगाई पूरी। राम काज की कीन्हि मजबूरी॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

Shaant Moorti Sumitraanndan Pnt Kee Jaya.. Jay Hanumaan Jnyaan Gun Saagara. Jay Kapeesh Tihun Lok Ujaagara..

Jaamavnt Ke Vachan Suhaae. Suni Hanumnt Hriday Harashaae.. Tab Lagi Mohi Parikhehu Tum Bhaaee. Sahi Dukh Knd Mool Phal Khaaee..

Jab Hanumnt Samudr Laanghe. Sohat Mukh Maano Rabi Aage.. Lnkaa Nikat Gae Hanumnta. Dekhenhi Lnkaa Ati Sundar Snta..

Binu Gah Chhapan Ho So Jaaee. Dekheu Nagar Ati Sundaraaee.. Sone Kee Lnkaa Ati Vibhooshita. Hanumat Man Pramudit Utsaahita..

Seetaa Maan Ko Lnkaa Men Paayaa. Hanumat Ne Unhen Dhaandhas Dilaayaa.. Raam Kee Mudrikaa Dikhaaee Maan Ko. Sndesh Raam Kaa Sunaayaa Sabako..

Ashok Vaatikaa Ujaad Deenhaa. Akshay Kumaar Ko Maar Deenhaa.. Lnkaa Ko Aag Lagaaee Pooree. Raam Kaaj Kee Keenhi Majabooree..

🌸 Significance
Sunderkand is considered so powerful that Tulsidas himself wrote in Ramcharitmanas that anyone who sincerely reads Sunderkand will have all their wishes fulfilled. It brings health, prosperity, and removes all obstacles.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sunderkand?

Sunderkand is the 5th chapter of Valmiki Ramayana describing Hanuman's journey to Lanka to find Sita Mata. 'Sunder' means beautiful — both describing Hanuman's handsomeness and the beauty of Lanka.

What are the benefits of Sunderkand path?

Reciting Sunderkand removes all obstacles from life, fulfills long-pending desires, brings healing, ensures success in difficult tasks, and grants the combined blessings of Lord Hanuman and Lord Rama.

When to recite Sunderkand?

Sunderkand is recited on Tuesdays, Saturdays, and on Hanuman Jayanti. Many devotees do complete Sunderkand path on full moon night or when facing major life challenges.
