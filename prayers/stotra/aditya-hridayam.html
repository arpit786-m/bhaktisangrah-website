Aditya Hridayam

आदित्य हृदयम्

Complete Stotram from Valmiki Ramayana — Surya Prayer

📚 Stotra 🪤 Lord Surya 🌐 Hindi + English

Aditya Hridayam (आदित्य हृदयम्) is a powerful hymn to Lord Surya (the Sun God) found in the Yuddha Kanda of Valmiki's Ramayana. The sage Agastya taught this prayer to Shri Ram before his battle with Ravana. Chanting Aditya Hridayam daily is said to remove all obstacles and ensure victory over all adversaries. It is one of the most complete and authentic solar prayers.

Aditya Hridayam — आदित्य हृदयम्

ततो युद्धपरिश्रांतं समरे चिन्तया स्थितम्। रावणं चाग्रतो दृष्ट्वा युद्धाय समुपस्थितम्॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

दैवतैश्च समागम्य द्रष्टुमभ्यागतो रणम्। उपागम्याब्रवीद् राममगस्त्यो भगवान् ऋषिः॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

राम राम महाबाहो श्रृणु गुह्यं सनातनम्। येन सर्वानरीन् वत्स समरे विजयिष्यसि॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

आदित्यहृदयं पुण्यं सर्वशत्रुविनाशनम्। जयावहं जपेन् नित्यम् अक्षय्यं परमं शिवम्॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

सर्वमंगलमांगल्यं सर्वपापप्रणाशनम्। चिन्ताशोकप्रशमनम् आयुर्वर्धनमुत्तमम्॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

रश्मिमन्तं समुद्यन्तं देवासुरनमस्कृतम्। पूजयस्व विवस्वन्तं भास्करं भुवनेश्वरम्॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

ॐ जय सूर्य देव, जय आदित्य नारायण। भास्कर भुवन भास्कर, जय जय सूर्य देव॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

Tato Yuddhaparishraantn Samare Chintayaa Sthitam. Raavann Chaagrato Drishtvaa Yuddhaay Samupasthitam..

Daivataishch Samaagamy Drashtumabhyaagato Ranam. Upaagamyaabraveed Raamamagastyo Bhagavaan Rishih..

Raam Raam Mahaabaaho Shrrinu Guhyn Sanaatanam. Yen Sarvaanareen Vats Samare Vijayishyasi..

Aadityahridayn Punyn Sarvashatruvinaashanam. Jayaavahn Japen Nityam Akshayyn Paramn Shivam..

Sarvamngalamaangalyn Sarvapaapapranaashanam. Chintaashokaprashamanam Aayurvardhanamuttamam..

Rashmimantn Samudyantn Devaasuranamaskritam. Poojayasv Vivasvantn Bhaaskarn Bhuvaneshvaram..

Om Jay Soory Deva, Jay Aadity Naaraayana. Bhaaskar Bhuvan Bhaaskara, Jay Jay Soory Deva..

🌸 Significance
Aditya Hridayam was the divine weapon given to Shri Ram — after chanting it, Ram was able to kill Ravana. Daily recitation grants health, victory, destroys all enemies, removes all sins, and energizes the body and mind like the Sun.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Aditya Hridayam?

Aditya Hridayam is an ancient Sanskrit hymn to Lord Surya (Sun God) from Valmiki Ramayana. The sage Agastya taught it to Sri Ram before his battle with Ravana to grant him divine energy and victory.

When to recite Aditya Hridayam?

Aditya Hridayam should be ideally recited at sunrise, facing the Sun (East direction). Sunday is the most auspicious day. Reciting it 3 times is traditional as mentioned in the text.

What are the benefits of Aditya Hridayam?

It destroys all enemies, removes all sins, brings health and longevity, ensures victory in all endeavors, eliminates grief and anxiety, and grants divine energy. It is said to be equivalent to performing a Yagna (fire sacrifice).
