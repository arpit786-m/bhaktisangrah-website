Kuber Mantra

कुबेर मंत्र

Om Yakshaya Kuberaya — Wealth God Mantra

📚 Mantra 🪤 Lord Kuber 🌐 Hindi + English

Kuber Mantra (कुबेर मंत्र) invokes Lord Kuber — the treasurer of gods and the lord of wealth (Dhan Adhipati). He rules the north direction and is the guardian of all earthly riches. His mantra is considered supremely powerful for attracting wealth, business success, and financial stability. Chanting on Diwali night or on Thursdays is most auspicious.

Kuber Mantra — कुबेर मंत्र

ॐ यक्षाय कुबेराय वैश्रवणाय धनधान्याधिपतये धनधान्यसमृद्धिं मे देहि दापय स्वाहा॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

ॐ श्रीं ह्रीं क्लीं श्रीं क्लीं वित्तेश्वराय नमः॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

ॐ कुबेराय नमः। ॐ धनाध्यक्षाय नमः। ॐ धनदाय नमः। ॐ वैश्रवणाय नमः॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

यक्षाय कुबेराय धनदाय नमो नमः। देव नमस्ते देव नमस्ते धन देव॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

Om Yakshaya Kuberaya Vaishravanaya Dhana-dhanyaadhipataye Dhana-dhanya-samriddhim Me Dehi Daapaya Svaha.

Om Shreem Hreem Kleem Shreem Kleem Vitteshwaraya Namah.

Om Kuberaya Namah. Om Dhanadhyakshaya Namah. Om Dhanadaya Namah. Om Vaishravanaya Namah.

🌸 Significance
Lord Kuber controls the flow of wealth in the universe. His mantra removes financial blockages, attracts new income sources, ensures business prosperity, and brings material abundance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Lord Kuber?

Lord Kuber (Kubera) is the Hindu god of wealth and the treasurer of the gods. He is the lord of the Yakshas and the ruler of the northern direction. He is also a devotee of Lord Shiva and guards the divine treasury.

How to chant Kuber Mantra?

Chant Kuber Mantra 108 times facing North, ideally on Thursdays or on Diwali night. Light a yellow/gold candle or diya. Place a gold coin in front while chanting for maximum effect.

What are the benefits of Kuber Mantra?

Kuber Mantra removes financial obstacles, attracts new wealth, ensures business growth, reduces debts, and brings Lord Kuber's direct blessings for material prosperity.
