मूल मंत्र — Gayatri Mantra

Lyrics in Hindi

ॐ भूर्भुवः स्वः
तत्सवितुर्वरेण्यं
भर्गो देवस्य धीमहि
धियो यो नः प्रचोदयात्॥

We meditate on the glory of the Creator;
Who has created the Universe;
Who is worthy of Worship;
Who is the embodiment of Knowledge and Light;
Who is the remover of all Sin and Ignorance;
May He enlighten our Intellect.

— ॥ —
शब्दार्थ — Word Meaning
  • Om (ॐ): The primeval sound, the Absolute.
  • Bhur (भूर्): The physical realm / Earth.
  • Bhuvah (भुवः): The mental realm / Atmosphere.
  • Svaha (स्वः): The spiritual realm / Heaven.
  • Tat (तत्): That (the Supreme entity).
  • Savitur (सवितुर्व): The Sun, the Creator and Source of all life.
  • Varenyam (रेण्यं): The most adorable, the highest, the most desirable.
  • Bhargo (भर्गो): Spiritual effulgence, illuminating light that destroys sins.
  • Devasya (देवस्य): Of the Divine / Supreme Lord.
  • Dheemahi (धीमहि): We meditate upon.
  • Dhiyo (धियो): The intellect, intelligence.
  • Yo (यो): Who.
  • Nah (नः): Our.
  • Prachodayat (प्रचोदयात्): May (He) guide, illumine, and inspire.
