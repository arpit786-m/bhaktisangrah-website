Lakshmi Mantra

लक्ष्मी मंत्र

Om Shreem Mahalakshmiyei Namah — Wealth Mantra

📚 Mantra 🪤 Goddess Lakshmi 🌐 Hindi + English

Lakshmi Mantra (लक्ष्मी मंत्र) invokes Goddess Lakshmi — the deity of wealth, fortune, beauty, and prosperity. The beej mantra 'Om Shreem Mahalakshmiyei Namah' is her most powerful seed mantra. Chanting on Fridays (Shukravar) and on Diwali brings financial blessings, removes debt, and attracts abundance.

Mahalakshmim cha vidmahe. Vishnupatnim cha dhimahi. Tanno Lakshmi prachodayat. (Lakshmi Gayatri Mantra)

🌸 Significance
Lakshmi Mantra removes poverty, attracts wealth and good fortune, brings beauty and harmony to the home, and ensures Goddess Lakshmi's presence and blessings in the devotee's life.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Lakshmi beej mantra?

'Om Shreem Mahalakshmiyei Namah' is the beej mantra of Goddess Lakshmi. 'Shreem' (श्रीं) is her beej akshar, representing divine grace and abundance.

How to chant Lakshmi Mantra?

Sit facing East or North, light a ghee diya, and chant 108 times on red coral beads or crystal mala. Friday evening (after sunset) is most auspicious.

What are the benefits of Lakshmi Mantra?

Regular chanting removes financial problems, attracts wealth, ensures job and business success, brings prosperity and comfort, and ensures Goddess Lakshmi never leaves your home.
