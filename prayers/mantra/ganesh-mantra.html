Ganesh Mantra

गणेश मंत्र

Om Gan Ganapataye Namah — Meaning & Benefits

📚 Mantra 🪤 Lord Ganesha 🌐 Hindi + English

Ganesh Mantra (गणेश मंत्र) invokes the blessings of Lord Ganesha — the remover of all obstacles and the lord of new beginnings. The powerful beej mantra 'Om Gan Ganapataye Namah' is one of the most effective mantras to chant before starting any new work. Ganesha is always worshipped first before any other deity.

Ekadantaya Vidmahe. Vakratundaya Dhimahi. Tanno Dantih Prachodayat. (Ganapati Gayatri)

🌸 Significance
Chanting Ganesh Mantra removes all obstacles (Vighnas) from the path of success, grants wisdom and intelligence, ensures auspicious beginnings, and provides Lord Ganesha's divine protection.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Om Gan Ganapataye Namah?

'Om Gan Ganapataye Namah' is the beej (seed) mantra of Lord Ganesha. 'Gan' is Ganesha's beej akshar. This mantra removes all obstacles and invites Ganesha's blessings before any auspicious work.

How many times to chant Ganesh Mantra?

Ganesh Mantra should be chanted 108 times daily using a rudraksha or wooden mala. Wednesday is the most auspicious day. Chanting 21 times before starting any work is also very effective.

What is Vakratunda Mahakaya?

Vakratunda Mahakaya Suryakoti Samaprabha is a shloka describing Lord Ganesha — 'one with a curved trunk, massive body, with the radiance of a million suns.' It is the most popular Ganesha prayer recited before any new venture.
