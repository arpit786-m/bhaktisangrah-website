Ram Mantra

राम मंत्र

Ram Ram — Sri Ram Jai Ram Jai Jai Ram Mantra

📚 Mantra 🪤 Lord Rama 🌐 Hindi + English

Ram Mantra (राम मंत्र) is the divine invocation of Lord Shri Ram — the ideal avatar of Lord Vishnu. According to Hanuman Chalisa, 'Ram naam' is the greatest name in the entire universe. The mantra 'Sri Ram Jai Ram Jai Jai Ram' was given by Saint Samarth Ramdas and purifies the mind completely with just 13 syllables.

Ram Mantra — राम मंत्र

श्री राम जय राम जय जय राम॥ श्री राम जय राम जय जय राम॥ श्री राम जय राम जय जय राम॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

राम राम राम राम राम राम राम राम। राम राम राम राम राम नाम तारनहार॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

ॐ रामाय नमः। रामचंद्राय नमः। रघुनंदनाय नमः। सीतापतये नमः। जानकीवल्लभाय नमः॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

रामं स्कंदं हनूमंतं वैनतेयं वृकोदरम्। शयने यः स्मरेन्नित्यं दुःस्वप्नं तस्य नश्यति॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

ॐ राम रामेति रामेति रमे रामे मनोरमे। सहस्रनाम तत्तुल्यं रामनाम वरानने॥ (भगवान शिव ने पार्वती को बताया — रामनाम हजार नामों के बराबर है)

⋯ ॐ ⋯

Sri Ram Jai Ram Jai Jai Ram! Sri Ram Jai Ram Jai Jai Ram! Sri Ram Jai Ram Jai Jai Ram!

Ram Ram Ram Ram Ram Ram Ram Ram. Ram Ram Ram Ram — Ram's name is the liberator.

Om Ramaya Namah. Ramachandraya Namah. Raghunandanaya Namah. Sitapataye Namah.

Om Ram Rameti Rameti Rame Rame Manorame. Sahasra-nama Tatthulyam Rama-nama Varanane. (Lord Shiva told Parvati — Ram's name equals 1000 divine names of Vishnu)

🌸 Significance
Chanting Ram Mantra removes all sins accumulated over many lifetimes, removes difficulties, grants divine protection, and fills the devotee with righteous energy and inner strength.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sri Ram Jai Ram Jai Jai Ram?

This 13-syllable mantra was given by Saint Samarth Ramdas Swami (Guru of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj) as the most powerful Ram mantra. It is called the Taraka Mantra (liberating mantra).

How many times to chant Ram Mantra?

Traditionally, Ram Mantra is chanted 108 times or in multiples: 108, 1008, or continuously for an hour. Tuesday mornings and Ram Navami are most auspicious.

Is Ram's name really equal to 1000 names of Vishnu?

Yes, according to a shloka narrated by Lord Shiva himself, chanting 'Ram' once equals chanting all 1000 names of Vishnu (Vishnu Sahasranama). This is why Ram Naam is considered the supreme mantra.
