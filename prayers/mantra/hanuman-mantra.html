Hanuman Mantra

हनुमान मंत्र

Powerful Mantras for Strength and Protection

📚 Mantra 🪤 Lord Hanuman 🌐 Hindi + English

Hanuman Mantra (हनुमान मंत्र) forms a powerful collection of mantras dedicated to Lord Hanuman — the embodiment of devotion, strength, and courage. These mantras provide protection from evil, grant physical and mental strength, remove fears, and ensure victory in all endeavours. Tuesdays and Saturdays are most auspicious for Hanuman mantras.

Hanuman Mantra — हनुमान मंत्र

ॐ हं हनुमते नमः॥ ॐ हं हनुमते रुद्रात्मकाय हुं फट्॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

मनोजवं मारुततुल्यवेगं जितेन्द्रियं बुद्धिमतां वरिष्ठम्। वातात्मजं वानरयूथमुख्यं श्रीरामदूतं शरणं प्रपद्ये॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

बुद्धिर्बलं यशो धैर्यं निर्भयत्वमरोगता। अजाड्यं वाक्पटुत्वं च हनुमत्स्मरणाद्भवेत्॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

ॐ नमो हनुमते रुद्रावताराय सर्वशत्रुसंहारणाय सर्वरोगहराय सर्वभय विनाशनाय सर्व विघ्न नाशाय सर्व कार्य सिद्धये ॐ हं पट् स्वाहा॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

जय हनुमान ज्ञान गुण सागर। जय कपीश तिहुं लोक उजागर॥ (हनुमान चालीसा का प्रथम दोहा)

⋯ ॐ ⋯

Om Hn Hanumate Namh.. Om Hn Hanumate Rudraatmakaay Hun Phat..

Manojavn Maarutatulyavegn Jitendriyn Buddhimataan Varishtham. Vaataatmajn Vaanarayoothamukhyn Shreeraamadootn Sharann Prapadye..

Buddhirbaln Yasho Dhairyn Nirbhayatvamarogataa. Ajaadyn Vaakpatutvn Ch Hanumatsmaranaadbhavet..

Om Namo Hanumate Rudraavataaraay Sarvashatrusnhaaranaay Sarvarogaharaay Sarvabhay Vinaashanaay Sarv Vighn Naashaay Sarv Kaary Siddhaye Om Hn Pat Svaahaa..

Jay Hanumaan Jnyaan Gun Saagara. Jay Kapeesh Tihun Lok Ujaagara.. (hanumaan Chaaleesaa Kaa Pratham Dohaa)

🌸 Significance
Lord Hanuman's mantras are known to be the most effective for removing evil influences, black magic, negative energies, and fears. Chanting gives the devotee Hanuman-like strength and fearlessness.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most powerful Hanuman mantra?

'Om Han Hanumate Namah' is considered the most powerful Hanuman beej mantra. For protection from evil, 'Om Namo Hanumate Rudraavataraaya' is highly effective. Hanuman Chalisa itself is also considered a complete mantra.

When to chant Hanuman Mantra?

Hanuman Mantra should be chanted on Tuesdays and Saturdays, ideally at sunrise or sunset. Chanting 108 times with full devotion brings maximum results.

What does Manojavam Marutatulyavegam mean?

This shloka means 'I take refuge in Lord Hanuman — swift as the mind, equal in speed to the wind, master of the senses, foremost among the wise, son of Vayu, chief among the monkey army, messenger of Sri Rama.'
