Ram Aarti

राम जी की आरती

Aarti Lyrics in Hindi — Jai Ram Ji Ki

📚 Aarti 🪤 Lord Rama 🌐 Hindi + English

Ram Aarti (राम जी की आरती) is a devotional aarti sung in praise of Lord Shri Ram, the seventh avatar of Lord Vishnu. Lord Ram is revered as the ideal king, son, husband, and devotee. This aarti is especially sung on Ram Navami and in Ram temples during the evening prayers. Chanting 'Jai Ram Ji Ki' fills the heart with peace and devotion.

🌸 Significance
Lord Rama is the embodiment of virtue, duty, and righteousness. Singing his aarti brings peace of mind, removes all fears, and blesses the devotee with a righteous and fulfilling life.

Frequently Asked Questions

When is Ram Aarti performed?

Ram Aarti is performed daily in Ram temples, especially during morning and evening prayers. It is most significant on Ram Navami, on Tuesdays, and after reading the Ramayana.

What is the significance of 'Jai Ram Ji Ki'?

'Jai Ram Ji Ki' means 'Victory to Lord Ram' — it is an affirmation of Lord Rama's greatness and an expression of devotion. Chanting this phrase brings positivity and divine blessings.

Who is Lord Rama?

Lord Rama is the seventh avatar (incarnation) of Lord Vishnu, born in Ayodhya. He is the central figure of the epic Ramayana and is revered as the ideal embodiment of dharma (righteousness), truth, and compassion.
