Vishnu Aarti

विष्णु जी की आरती

Om Jai Jagdish Hare — Full Aarti Lyrics in Hindi

📚 Aarti 🪤 Lord Vishnu 🌐 Hindi + English

Vishnu Aarti, popularly known as 'Om Jai Jagdish Hare' (ॐ जय जगदीश हरे), is the most widely sung aarti in Hindu households across India. Originally a prayer to Lord Vishnu (Jagdish = Lord of the Universe), it is sung daily in millions of homes as the evening aarti. It was composed by Pandit Shradha Ram Phillouri in 1870.

Vishnu Aarti — विष्णु जी की आरती

ॐ जय जगदीश हरे, स्वामी जय जगदीश हरे। भक्त जनों के संकट, दास जनों के संकट, क्षण में दूर करे॥ ॐ जय जगदीश हरे॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

जो ध्यावे फल पावे, दुख विनशे मन का। स्वामी दुख विनशे मन का। सुख सम्पत्ति घर आवे, कष्ट मिटे तन का॥ ॐ जय जगदीश हरे॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

मात पिता तुम मेरे, शरण गहूं किसकी। स्वामी शरण गहूं किसकी। तुम बिन और न दूजा, आस करूं जिसकी॥ ॐ जय जगदीश हरे॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

तुम पूर्ण परमात्मा, तुम अंतर्यामी। स्वामी तुम अंतर्यामी। पार ब्रह्म परमेश्वर, तुम सब के स्वामी॥ ॐ जय जगदीश हरे॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

तुम करुणा के सागर, तुम पालनकर्ता। स्वामी तुम पालनकर्ता। मैं मूरख अज्ञानी, कृपा करो स्वामी॥ ॐ जय जगदीश हरे॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

तुम हो एक अगोचर, सब के प्राणपति। स्वामी सब के प्राणपति। किस विधि मिलूं दयामय, तुमको मैं कुमति॥ ॐ जय जगदीश हरे॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

Om Jai Jagdish Hare, Swami Jai Jagdish Hare. Bhakt jano ke sankat, Das jano ke sankat, Kshan mein door kare. Om Jai Jagdish Hare.

Jo dhyave phal pave, Dukh vinashe man ka. Swami dukh vinashe man ka. Sukh sampatti ghar aave, Kasht mite tan ka. Om Jai Jagdish Hare.

🌸 Significance
Om Jai Jagdish Hare is the quintessential Hindu household prayer. Sung every evening, it creates divine vibrations in the home, purifies the environment, and invokes Lord Vishnu's protection and blessings for the entire family.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who composed Om Jai Jagdish Hare?

Om Jai Jagdish Hare was composed by Pandit Shradha Ram Phillouri in 1870. It was originally a prayer to Lord Vishnu and has since become the most popular daily household aarti in India.

What does Jagdish mean?

Jagdish (जगदीश) means 'Lord of the Universe' — Jag means universe and Ish means lord/god. It is a name for Lord Vishnu as the supreme ruler of all creation.

When is Vishnu Aarti performed?

Vishnu Aarti is traditionally performed every day at sunset (evening prayer/sandhya). It is also sung on Ekadashi, Thursdays, and during all Vishnu-related festivals.
