Vaishno Devi Aarti

वैष्णो देवी की आरती

Jai Vaishno Devi Mata Ki Aarti in Hindi

📚 Aarti 🪤 Maa Vaishno Devi 🌐 Hindi + English

Vaishno Devi Aarti (वैष्णो देवी की आरती) is a devotional prayer to Maa Vaishno Devi, the supreme goddess enshrined in the holy cave of Trikuta Mountains in Katra, Jammu. Maa Vaishno Devi is considered a manifestation of the Supreme Goddess Adishakti. Millions of devotees undertake the holy pilgrimage (yatra) to seek her blessings every year.

Vaishno Devi Aarti — वैष्णो देवी की आरती

जय माँ वैष्णो देवी, जय माँ वैष्णो देवी। त्रिकुटे विराजती माँ, जय माँ वैष्णो देवी॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

कोटि-कोटि रूप धरे माँ, कोटि नाम तेरे। भक्तों की पुकार सुनती, संकट हर लेती माँ॥ जय माँ वैष्णो देवी, जय माँ वैष्णो देवी।

⋯ ॐ ⋯

पिंडी के रूप में विराजे, तेरे तीन रूप माँ। महाकाली, महालक्ष्मी, महासरस्वती माँ॥ जय माँ वैष्णो देवी, जय माँ वैष्णो देवी।

⋯ ॐ ⋯

अर्धकुँवारी के दर्शन से, पाप सभी कट जाते। भैरव नाथ की पूजा से, दर्शन पूर्ण होते॥ जय माँ वैष्णो देवी, जय माँ वैष्णो देवी।

⋯ ॐ ⋯

जो भी आए तेरे द्वारे, खाली न जाए कोई। जय माता दी का नारा, गूंजे घर घर डोलता॥ जय माँ वैष्णो देवी, जय माँ वैष्णो देवी।

Ardhakunvaaree Ke Darshan Se, Paap Sabhee Kat Jaate. Bhairav Naath Kee Poojaa Se, Darshan Poorn Hote.. Jay Maan Vaishno Devee, Jay Maan Vaishno Devee.

Jo Bhee Aae Tere Dvaare, Khaalee N Jaae Koee. Jay Maataa Dee Kaa Naaraa, Goonje Ghar Ghar Dolataa.. Jay Maan Vaishno Devee, Jay Maan Vaishno Devee.

🌸 Significance
Maa Vaishno Devi fulfills all wishes of her devoted children. Her blessings remove all obstacles and hardships from life. Singing her aarti with pure devotion is said to be equivalent to completing her sacred pilgrimage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Vaishno Devi temple located?

Maa Vaishno Devi temple is located in the Trikuta Mountains, near Katra town in Jammu district of Jammu and Kashmir, India. The holy cave shrine is at an altitude of 5,200 feet above sea level.

What is the significance of Vaishno Devi?

Maa Vaishno Devi is considered one of the most sacred shrines in India. The goddess is a combined manifestation of Goddess Kali, Goddess Lakshmi, and Goddess Saraswati — known as Tridevi.

How many visitors come to Vaishno Devi each year?

The Vaishno Devi shrine receives over 8 million pilgrims annually, making it one of the most visited religious shrines in the world.
