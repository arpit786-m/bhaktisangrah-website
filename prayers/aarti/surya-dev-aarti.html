Surya Dev Aarti

सूर्य देव की आरती

Om Jai Surya Bhagwan — Aarti Lyrics in Hindi

📚 Aarti 🪤 Lord Surya 🌐 Hindi + English

Surya Dev Aarti (सूर्य देव की आरती) is a devotional aarti sung in praise of Lord Surya — the Sun God. Surya is the visible form of the divine, the source of all life and light. Singing his aarti at sunrise on Sundays energizes the body, mind, and soul with divine solar energy.

Surya Dev Aarti — सूर्य देव की आरती

ॐ जय सूर्य भगवान, प्रभु जय सूर्य भगवान। गायत्री के वरदाता, ज्ञान प्रकाशकारण॥ ॐ जय सूर्य भगवान॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

सप्त अश्व रथ पर सवार, अरुण सारथी साथ। तीनों लोक प्रकाशक तुम, जग के हो जगदाता॥ ॐ जय सूर्य भगवान॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

तुम्हारे रश्मि से ही जग, जीवन पाता जीव। आरोग्य देते हो सबको, भक्तों के तुम दीव॥ ॐ जय सूर्य भगवान॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

रविवार को व्रत करके, जल चढ़ाएं प्रभु। नेत्र ज्योति और आरोग्य, मिले भक्तों को नित्य॥ ॐ जय सूर्य भगवान॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

Om Jay Soory Bhagavaana, Prabhu Jay Soory Bhagavaana. Gaayatree Ke Varadaataa, Jnyaan Prakaashakaarana.. Om Jay Soory Bhagavaana..

Sapt Ashv Rath Par Savaara, Arun Saarathee Saatha. Teenon Lok Prakaashak Tuma, Jag Ke Ho Jagadaataa.. Om Jay Soory Bhagavaana..

Tumhaare Rashmi Se Hee Jaga, Jeevan Paataa Jeeva. Aarogy Dete Ho Sabako, Bhakton Ke Tum Deeva.. Om Jay Soory Bhagavaana..

Ravivaar Ko Vrat Karake, Jal Chadhaaen Prabhu. Netr Jyoti Aur Aarogya, Mile Bhakton Ko Nitya.. Om Jay Soory Bhagavaana..

🌸 Significance
Lord Surya grants health, vitality, willpower, and fame. His aarti removes chronic diseases, bestows excellent eyesight, and ensures success in all endeavors.

Frequently Asked Questions

When is Surya Dev Aarti performed?

Surya Dev Aarti is performed at sunrise on Sundays (Ravivar). Devotees offer water (Arghya) to the rising sun and then sing the aarti. It can also be recited during Chhath Puja.

Why is Sunday sacred for Lord Surya?

Sunday (Ravivar) is dedicated to Lord Surya as 'Ravi' is another name for Surya. Fasting, chanting, and worshipping Surya on Sundays increases vitality, cures diseases, and brings his blessings.

What is Chhath Puja?

Chhath Puja is a major festival dedicated to Lord Surya, celebrated mainly in Bihar, UP, and Jharkhand. Devotees observe a strict fast and offer arghya to the setting and rising sun over 4 days.
