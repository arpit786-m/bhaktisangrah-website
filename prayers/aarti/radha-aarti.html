Radha Rani Aarti

राधा रानी की आरती

Aarti Radha Rani Ki — Full Lyrics in Hindi

📚 Aarti 🪤 Radha Rani 🌐 Hindi + English

Radha Rani Aarti (राधा रानी की आरती) is a devotional aarti sung in praise of Shri Radha Rani — the divine consort of Lord Krishna and the embodiment of pure love and devotion. She is considered the highest form of Bhakti (devotion) and is the most beloved of Krishna. Barsana (Mathura) is her birthplace and pilgrimage center.

Radha Rani Aarti — राधा रानी की आरती

🌸 Significance
Radha Rani is the gateway to Krishna — devotees believe that Radha's grace leads directly to Krishna's grace. Singing her aarti fills the heart with divine love and bhakti.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Radha Rani?

Radha Rani (Shri Radha) is the divine consort of Lord Krishna and the embodiment of pure Bhakti. She is worshipped as the foremost devotee of Krishna and the one who loves him most perfectly.

Where is Radha Rani's temple?

The main Radha Rani temple is at Barsana in Mathura district, Uttar Pradesh. Barsana is considered Radha's birthplace and the 'Lathmar Holi' festival there is world-famous.

When is Radha Aarti most auspicious?

Radha Aarti is sung at morning and evening puja in Vaishnava temples. It is most auspicious on Radha Ashtami (Radha's birthday), on Janmashtami, and during Holi celebrations at Barsana.
