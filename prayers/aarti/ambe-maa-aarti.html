Ambe Maa Aarti

अम्बे माँ की आरती

Jai Jai Ambe Jai Jagdambe — Full Aarti Lyrics

📚 Aarti 🪤 Maa Ambe 🌐 Hindi + English

Ambe Maa Aarti (अम्बे माँ की आरती) is a beautiful devotional aarti sung in praise of Maa Ambe — another name for Goddess Durga, the universal mother. This aarti is a favourite during Navratri, Garba celebrations, and Devi puja. It captures the goddess's tender motherly aspect who protects and nurtures all her devotees.

🌸 Significance
Singing Ambe Maa Aarti during Navratri fills the atmosphere with divine feminine energy. The goddess removes all afflictions and bestows her children with strength, health, and prosperity.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ambe Maa Aarti?

Ambe Maa Aarti is a devotional aarti to Goddess Durga (also called Ambe, meaning 'Mother'). It is especially popular during Navratri and Garba celebrations in Gujarat and across India.

What is the difference between Durga Aarti and Ambe Maa Aarti?

Both are addressed to Goddess Durga. 'Ambe Maa' emphasizes the tender, motherly aspect of the goddess, while Durga Aarti focuses more on her warrior form. Ambe Maa is very popular in Gujarati tradition.

When is Ambe Maa Aarti sung?

Ambe Maa Aarti is sung during all 9 days of Navratri, especially during Garba nights. It is also sung during Devi puja, on Fridays, and at any time of trouble when seeking the mother's protection.
