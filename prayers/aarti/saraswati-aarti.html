सरस्वती आरती — Jai Saraswati Mata

Lyrics in Hindi

जय सरस्वती माता, मैया जय सरस्वती माता।
सद्गुण वैभव शालिनी, त्रिभुवन विख्याता॥ जय सरस्वती माता॥

चन्द्रवदनि पद्मासिनी, द्युति मंगलकारी।
सोहे शुभ हंस सवारी, अतुल तेजधारी॥ जय सरस्वती माता॥

बायें कर में वीणा, दायें कर माला।
शीश मुकुट मणि सोहे, गल मोतियन माला॥ जय सरस्वती माता॥

देवि शरण जो आये, उनका उद्धार किया।
पैठी दुष्टों के हृदय, उनका संहार किया॥ जय सरस्वती माता॥

विद्या ज्ञान प्रदायिनि, ज्ञान प्रकाश भरो।
मोह अज्ञान तिमिर का, जग से नाश करो॥ जय सरस्वती माता॥

धूप दीप फल मेवा, माँ स्वीकार करो।
ज्ञानचक्षु दे माता, जग निस्तार करो॥ जय सरस्वती माता॥

माँ सरस्वती की आरती, जो कोई जन गावे।
हितकारी सुखकारी, ज्ञान भक्ति पावे॥ जय सरस्वती माता॥

Jay Saraswati Mata, Maiya Jay Saraswati Mata,
Sadgun Vaibhav Shaalini, Tribhuvan Vikhyaata. Jay Saraswati Mata.

Chandravadani Padmaasini, Dyuti Mangalkaari,
Sohe Shubh Hans Sawaari, Atul Tejdhaari. Jay Saraswati Mata.

Baaye Kar Mein Veena, Daaye Kar Maala,
Sheesh Mukut Mani Sohe, Gal Motiyan Mala. Jay Saraswati Mata.

Devi Sharan Jo Aaye, Unka Uddhar Kiya,
Paithi Dushton Ke Hriday, Unka Sanhaar Kiya. Jay Saraswati Mata.

Vidya Gyaan Pradaayini, Gyaan Prakaash Bharo,
Moh Agyan Timir Ka, Jag Se Naash Karo. Jay Saraswati Mata.

Dhoop Deep Phal Mewa, Maan Sweekar Karo,
Gyaanchakshu De Mata, Jag Nistaar Karo. Jay Saraswati Mata.

Maan Saraswati Ki Aarti, Jo Koi Jan Gaave,
Hitkaaree Sukhkaaree, Gyaan Bhakti Paave. Jay Saraswati Mata.

Victory to Mother Saraswati! Victory to Divine Mother Saraswati!
Endowed with virtues and glory, renowned in all three worlds. Victory to Saraswati.

With a moon-like face, seated on a lotus, your splendor brings auspiciousness.
You ride a beautiful swan, possessing incomparable radiance. Victory to Saraswati.

Holding the Veena in your left hand, and a rosary in your right.
A jewelled crown adorns your head, and a pearl garland graces your neck. Victory to Saraswati.

O Goddess, whoever takes refuge in you is uplifted.
Entering the hearts of the wicked, you destroy their evil. Victory to Saraswati.

Bestower of true education and knowledge, fill us with the light of wisdom.
Destroy the darkness of ignorance and delusion from the world. Victory to Saraswati.

Accept our offerings of incense, lamp, fruits, and sweets, O Mother.
Grant us the eye of wisdom and liberate the world. Victory to Saraswati.

Whoever sings this Aarti of Mother Saraswati —
Finds it beneficial, joyful, and attains knowledge and devotion. Victory to Saraswati.

About Saraswati Aarti: Goddess Saraswati is the Hindu deity of knowledge, music, art, speech, wisdom, and learning. She is part of the Tridevi (Saraswati, Lakshmi, and Parvati). This Aarti is sung to seek her blessings for clarity of thought, eloquence, academic excellence, and mastery over arts.
