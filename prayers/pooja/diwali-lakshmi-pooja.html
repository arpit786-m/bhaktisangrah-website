Diwali Lakshmi Pooja Vidhi

दीवाली लक्ष्मी पूजा विधि

Complete Step-by-Step Diwali Puja Guide in Hindi

📚 Pooja 🪤 Goddess Lakshmi 🌐 Hindi + English

Diwali Lakshmi Puja (दीवाली लक्ष्मी पूजा) is the most auspicious puja performed on the night of Diwali (Amavasya of Kartik month) to invite Goddess Lakshmi into the home. This puja brings wealth, prosperity, and removes all financial obstacles for the entire coming year. It is performed at a specific auspicious time (muhurat) after sunset.

Diwali Lakshmi Pooja Vidhi — दीवाली लक्ष्मी पूजा विधि

🪔 पूजा सामग्री: कमल या गुलाब फूल, रोली, चावल, सुपारी, नारियल, घी का दीपक, अगरबत्ती, मिठाई, धनिया, लक्ष्मी जी की मूर्ति/तस्वीर, सिक्के, चांदी/सोना।

⋯ ॐ ⋯

🙏 पूजा विधि — स्थापना: स्वच्छ स्थान पर लाल कपड़ा बिछाएं। लक्ष्मी-गणेश की मूर्ति स्थापित करें। कलश रखें। दीपक जलाएं। फूल-माला से सजाएं।

⋯ ॐ ⋯

🌸 आचमन और संकल्प: जल से आचमन करें। संकल्प लें: 'ॐ अद्य कार्तिक अमावस्यायां श्री महालक्ष्मी प्रसन्नार्थं पूजनं करिष्ये।'

⋯ ॐ ⋯

🏵️ पूजन मंत्र: ॐ महालक्ष्म्यै नमः। (16 बार पुष्प अर्पित करें) ॐ श्रीं ह्रीं श्रीं महालक्ष्म्यै नमः। नमस्ते अस्तु महामाये श्रीपीठे सुरपूजिते। शंखचक्रगदाहस्ते महालक्ष्मी नमोऽस्तु ते॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

🪔 आरती और समापन: लक्ष्मी आरती गाएं। प्रसाद वितरित करें। पूजन के बाद घर के मुख्य द्वार पर दीप जलाएं। रात भर दीपक जलते रहने दें।

⋯ ॐ ⋯

🪔 Poojaa Saamagree: Kamal Yaa Gulaab Phoola, Rolee, Chaavala, Supaaree, Naariyala, Ghee Kaa Deepaka, Agarabattee, Mithaaee, Dhaniyaa, Lakshmee Jee Kee Moorti/tasveera, Sikke, Chaandee/sonaa.

🙏 Poojaa Vidhi — Sthaapanaa: Svachchh Sthaan Par Laal Kapadaa Bichhaaen. Lakshmee-ganesh Kee Moorti Sthaapit Karen. Kalash Rakhen. Deepak Jalaaen. Phoola-maalaa Se Sajaaen.

🌸 Aachaman Aur Snkalpa: Jal Se Aachaman Karen. Snkalp Len: 'Om Ady Kaartik Amaavasyaayaan Shree Mahaalakshmee Prasannaarthn Poojann Karishye.'

🏵️ Poojan Mntra: Om Mahaalakshmyai Namh. (16 Baar Pushp Arpit Karen) Om Shreen Hreen Shreen Mahaalakshmyai Namh. Namaste Astu Mahaamaaye Shreepeethe Surapoojite. Shnkhachakragadaahaste Mahaalakshmee Namoastu Te..

🪔 Aaratee Aur Samaapana: Lakshmee Aaratee Gaaen. Prasaad Vitarit Karen. Poojan Ke Baad Ghar Ke Mukhy Dvaar Par Deep Jalaaen. Raat Bhar Deepak Jalate Rahane Den.

🌸 Significance
Performing Lakshmi Puja on Diwali night opens the doors of wealth and prosperity. It is believed that Goddess Lakshmi herself visits homes that are clean, decorated, and where sincere puja is performed.

Frequently Asked Questions

What time to perform Lakshmi Puja on Diwali?

Diwali Lakshmi Puja should be performed during Pradosh Kaal (after sunset) and during the Amavasya tithi. The exact muhurat varies by year and location. In 2026, Diwali falls on November 3.

What items are needed for Diwali Lakshmi Puja?

You need: Lakshmi-Ganesha idols, red cloth, kalash (pot with water), mango leaves, coconut, roli, rice, flowers (lotus or marigold), sweets, ghee lamps, incense, turmeric, vermilion, and some coins or silver items.

Why is Ganesha worshipped first on Diwali?

Lord Ganesha is always worshipped before any other deity to remove obstacles and ensure a successful puja. On Diwali, Ganesha and Lakshmi are worshipped together as they represent prosperity and auspicious beginnings.
