Ganesh Chalisa

गणेश चालीसा

Full 40 Verses in Hindi with Meaning

📚 Chalisa 🪤 Lord Ganesha 🌐 Hindi + English

Ganesh Chalisa (गणेश चालीसा) is a hymn of 40 verses glorifying Lord Ganesha, the elephant-headed deity who is the remover of all obstacles and the lord of beginnings. Before any auspicious work, worship of Lord Ganesha is considered essential. This chalisa is recited on Wednesdays and on Ganesh Chaturthi for blessings.

Ganesh Chalisa — गणेश चालीसा

जय गणपति सदगुण सदन, कविवर बदन कृपाल। विघ्न हरण मंगल करण, जय जय गिरिजालाल॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

जय जय जय गणपति गणराजू। मंगल भरण करण शुभ काजू॥ जय गजबदन सदन सुखदाता। विश्व विनायक बुद्धि विधाता॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

वक्र तुण्ड शुचि शुण्ड सुहावन। तिलक त्रिपुण्ड भाल मन भावन॥ राजत मणि मुक्तन उर माला। स्वर्ण मुकुट शिर नयन विशाला॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

पुस्तक पाणि कुठार त्रिशूलं। मोदक भोग सुगंध फूलं॥ सुन्दर पीताम्बर तन साजित। चरण पादुका मुनि मन राजित॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

धनी शक्ति बल बुद्धि निधाना। सुत पार्वती पति गुणखाना॥ ऋद्धि सिद्धि तव चंवर सुधारें। मूषक वाहन सोहत द्वारें॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

कहौं जन्म शुभ कथा तुम्हारी। अति शुचि पावन मंगलकारी॥ एक समय गिरिराज कुमारी। पुत्र हेतु तप कीन्हो भारी॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

भयो यज्ञ जब पूर्ण अनूपा। तब पहुँचे तुम धरि द्विज रूपा॥ अतिथि जानि कै गौरि सुखारी। बहुविधि सेवा करी तुम्हारी॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

अति प्रसन्न ह्वै तुम वर दीन्हा। मातु पुत्र हित जो तप कीन्हा॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

Jay Ganapati Sadagun Sadana, Kavivar Badan Kripaala. Vighn Haran Mngal Karana, Jay Jay Girijaalaala..

Jay Jay Jay Ganapati Ganaraajoo. Mngal Bharan Karan Shubh Kaajoo.. Jay Gajabadan Sadan Sukhadaataa. Vishv Vinaayak Buddhi Vidhaataa..

Vakr Tund Shuchi Shund Suhaavana. Tilak Tripund Bhaal Man Bhaavana.. Raajat Mani Muktan Ur Maalaa. Svarn Mukut Shir Nayan Vishaalaa..

Pustak Paani Kuthaar Trishooln. Modak Bhog Sugndh Phooln.. Sundar Peetaambar Tan Saajita. Charan Paadukaa Muni Man Raajita..

Dhanee Shakti Bal Buddhi Nidhaanaa. Sut Paarvatee Pati Gunakhaanaa.. Riddhi Siddhi Tav Chnvar Sudhaaren. Mooshak Vaahan Sohat Dvaaren..

Kahaun Janm Shubh Kathaa Tumhaaree. Ati Shuchi Paavan Mngalakaaree.. Ek Samay Giriraaj Kumaaree. Putr Hetu Tap Keenho Bhaaree..

Bhayo Yajny Jab Poorn Anoopaa. Tab Pahunche Tum Dhari Dvij Roopaa.. Atithi Jaani Kai Gauri Sukhaaree. Bahuvidhi Sevaa Karee Tumhaaree..

Ati Prasann Hvai Tum Var Deenhaa. Maatu Putr Hit Jo Tap Keenhaa..

🌸 Significance
Reciting Ganesh Chalisa before any new beginning — whether starting a business, studies, or journey — invokes Lord Ganesha's blessings who removes all obstacles from the path.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ganesh Chalisa?

Ganesh Chalisa is a 40-verse prayer dedicated to Lord Ganesha, the son of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati. It praises his divine qualities and seeks his blessings for success and removal of obstacles.

When should Ganesh Chalisa be recited?

Ganesh Chalisa is most auspicious on Wednesdays (Budhwar) and especially on Ganesh Chaturthi. It should also be recited before starting any important task or journey.

What are the benefits of Ganesh Chalisa?

Regular recitation of Ganesh Chalisa removes obstacles in life, brings success in new ventures, improves intelligence and wisdom, and grants the blessings of Lord Ganesha.
