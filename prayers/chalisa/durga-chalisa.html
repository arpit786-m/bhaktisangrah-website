शुरुवात — Introduction

Lyrics in Hindi

नमो नमो दुर्गे सुख करनी। नमो नमो अम्बे दुःख हरनी॥
निराकार है ज्योति तुम्हारी। तिहूँ लोक फैली उजियारी॥

I bow down to you, O Goddess Durga, the bestower of happiness. I bow down to you, O Goddess Amba, the destroyer of sorrow.
Your divine light has no form, yet its radiance spreads across the three worlds.

— ॥ —
चौपाई — Chaupai

Lyrics in Hindi

शशि ललाट मुख महाविशाला। नेत्र लाल भृकुटि विकराला॥
रूप मातु को अधिक सुहावे। दरश करत जन अति सुख पावे॥

तुम संसार शक्ति लै कीना। पालन हेतु अन्न धन दीना॥
अन्नपूर्णा हुई जग पाला। तुम ही आदि सुन्दरी बाला॥

प्रलयकाल सब नाशन हारी। तुम गौरी शिवसंकर प्यारी॥
शिव योगी तुम्हरे गुण गावें। ब्रह्मा विष्णु तुम्हें नित ध्यावें॥

रूप सरस्वती को तुम धारा। दे सद्बुद्धि ऋषि मुनिन उबारा॥
धरयो रूप नरसिंह को अम्बा। परगट भई फाड़कर खम्बा॥

रक्षा करि प्रह्लाद बचायो। हिरण्याक्ष को स्वर्ग पठायो॥
लक्ष्मी रूप धरो जग माहीं। श्री नारायण अंग समाहीं॥

क्षीरसिन्धु में करत विलासा। दयासिन्धु दीजै मन आसा॥
हिंगलाज में तुम्हीं भवानी। महिमा अमित न जात बखानी॥

मातंगी अरु धूमावति माता। भुवनेश्वरी बगला सुख दाता॥
श्री भैरव तारा जग तारिणी। छिन्न भाल भव दुःख निवारिणी॥

केहरि वाहन सोह भवानी। लांगुर वीर चलत अगवानी॥
कर में खप्पर खड्ग विराजे। जाको देख काल डर भाजे॥

सोहै अस्त्र और त्रिशूला। जाते उठत शत्रु हिय शूला॥
नगर कोटि में तुम्हीं विराजत। तिहुँलोक में डंका बाजत॥

शुम्भ निशुम्भ दानव तुम मारे। रक्तबीज शंखन संहारे॥
महिषासुर नृप अति अभिमानी। जेहि अघ भार मही अकुलानी॥

रूप कराल कालिका धारा। सेन सहित तुम तिहि संहारा॥
परी गाढ़ सन्तन पर जब जब। भई सहाय मातु तुम तब तब॥

अमरपुरी अरु बासव लोका। तब महिमा सब रहें अशोका॥
ज्वाला में है ज्योति तुम्हारी। तुम्हें सदा पूजें नर-नारी॥

प्रेम भक्ति से जो यश गावें। दुःख दारिद्र निकट नहिं आवें॥
ध्यावे तुम्हें जो नर मन लाई। जन्म-मरण ताकौ छुटि जाई॥

जोगी सुर मुनि कहत पुकारी। योग न हो बिन शक्ति तुम्हारी॥
शंकर आचारज तप कीनो। काम अरु क्रोध जीति सब लीनो॥

निशिदिन ध्यान धरो शंकर को। काहु काल नहिं सुमिरो तुमको॥
शक्ति रूप को मरम न पायो। शक्ति गई तब मन पछतायो॥

शरणागत हुई कीर्ति बखानी। जय जय जय जगदम्ब भवानी॥
भई प्रसन्न आदि जगदम्बा। दई शक्ति नहिं कीन विलम्बा॥

मोको मातु कष्ट अति घेरो। तुम बिन कौन हरै दुःख मेरो॥
आशा तृष्णा निपट सतावें। मोह मदान्दिक सब बिनशावें॥

शत्रु नाश कीजै महारानी। सुमिरौं इकचित तुम्हें भवानी॥
करो कृपा हे मातु दयाला। ऋद्धि-सिद्धि दै करहु निहाला॥

जब लगि जिऊँ दया फल पाऊँ। तुम्हरो यश मैं सदा सुनाऊँ॥
दुर्गा चालीसा जो कोई गावै। सब सुख भोग परमपद पावै॥

देवीदास शरण निज जानी। करहु कृपा जगदम्ब भवानी॥

The moon adorns your forehead, and your face is vast. Your eyes are red, and your frown is terrifying.
Your beautiful form is highly pleasing; devotees find great joy just seeing you.

With your power, you created the world; you provide food and wealth for its sustenance.
As Annapurna, you nourish the universe; you are the primordial, eternal youthful beauty.

At the time of dissolution, you destroy everything; you are Gauri, beloved of Lord Shiva.
Lord Shiva, the great Yogi, sings your praises; Brahma and Vishnu constantly meditate upon you.

Taking the form of Saraswati, you grant supreme wisdom and save the sages.
O Amba, you took the form of Narasimha, tearing open the pillar to appear.

You protected Prahlad and saved him, while sending Hiranyaksha to the afterlife.
You take the form of Lakshmi on earth, residing eternally by the side of Lord Narayana.

You reside joyfully in the ocean of milk; O Ocean of Mercy, fulfill my heart's desires.
You are the Goddess manifesting in Hingalaj; your glory is immense and beyond description.

You are Matangi, Dhumavati, Bhuvaneshwari, and Bagalamukhi, bestowers of happiness.
You are Bhairavi and Tara, saviour of the world; you are Chinnamasta, who removes worldly sorrows.

Riding the lion, O Goddess Bhavani, brave Lord Hanuman leads the way before you.
Holding a skull and a sword in your hands, even Death (Kala) runs away in fear upon seeing you.

You are adorned with weapons and a trident, sight of which strikes terror in the hearts of enemies.
You reside in Nagarkot (Kangra), and your power resounds throughout the three worlds.

You killed the demons Shumbha and Nishumbha, and destroyed Raktabija and numerous others.
When the highly arrogant demon king Mahishasura burdened the earth with his sins,

You took on the fierce form of Kali, and destroyed him along with his entire army.
Whenever the saints and sages have faced difficulties, O Mother, you have always rushed to help.

In Amarpuri (heaven) and across all realms, your greatness keeps all beings free from sorrow.
Your divine flame burns endlessly in Jwala Ji; men and women constantly worship you.

Those who sing your glory with love and devotion are never touched by sorrow or poverty.
Whoever meditates upon you with full focus is liberated from the cycle of birth and death.

Yogis, gods, and sages exclaim loudly that no meditation (Yoga) is possible without your Shakti (energy).
Adi Shankaracharya performed severe penance, conquering both lust and anger.

He meditated daily only upon Lord Shiva, never devoting time to worship you.
Not understanding the essence of your Supreme Power (Shakti), he lost his strength and greatly repented.

He then surrendered to you and sang your praises: "Victory, victory, victory to Jagadamba, the Mother of the Universe."
The primordial Mother was pleased; she immediately granted him his strength back without delay.

O Mother, immense troubles surround me; who else but you can remove my sorrows?
Unending desires and thirst constantly torment me; arrogance, attachment, and ego destroy my peace.

Destroy my enemies, O Empress; I focus my heart solely on you, O Goddess Bhavani.
Be merciful, O compassionate Mother! Bless me with Riddhi and Siddhi (spiritual and material wealth) and fulfill my life.

As long as I live, may I receive your mercy, and I will constantly narrate your glorious deeds to the world.
Whoever sings this Durga Chalisa will enjoy all earthly pleasures and ultimately attain liberation.

Knowing him to be your devotee seeking refuge, please bestow your grace upon Devidas, O Mother of the Universe.

About Durga Chalisa: Composed by Devidas, this forty-verse invocation praises Durga as the supreme cosmic power (Shakti). It recounts all her mythological victories over demons and seeks her protection against life's tribulations and inner enemies like anger and ego.
