Lakshmi Chalisa

लक्ष्मी चालीसा

Full 40 Verses in Hindi with Meaning

📚 Chalisa 🪤 Goddess Lakshmi 🌐 Hindi + English

Lakshmi Chalisa (लक्ष्मी चालीसा) is a 40-verse devotional hymn dedicated to Goddess Lakshmi, the goddess of wealth, fortune, and prosperity. Reciting Lakshmi Chalisa on Fridays and on Diwali invites the goddess's blessings into the home. She is the consort of Lord Vishnu and represents abundance, grace, and beauty.

Lakshmi Chalisa — लक्ष्मी चालीसा

मातु लक्ष्मी करि कृपा, करो हृदय में वास। मनोकामना सिद्ध कर, पुरवहु मेरी आस॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

जय जय जय लक्ष्मी महारानी। सब की तुम हो शुभ कल्याणी॥ विष्णु प्रिया प्रकृति भवानी। सत्य सनातन सुखदायानी॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

कमला, कमलासन वासिनी। ज्ञान विज्ञान विलासिनी॥ सदा संपत्ति की दायिनी। भक्तों की दुख नाशिनी॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

श्वेत अम्बर धारण करती। सोने की आभा बिखरती॥ कमल पुष्प पर विराजती। भक्तों के घर शोभा लाती॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

लक्ष्मी जी की आरती कीजे। धन-धान्य घर में भर लीजे॥ शुक्रवार व्रत जो करे भाई। लक्ष्मी माता की कृपा पाई॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

जो यह चालीसा नित पढ़ैं। धन संपत्ति सुख-समृद्धि बढ़ैं॥ लक्ष्मी जी कृपा करें तब। संकट मिटे सुख पाए सब॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

Maatu Lakshmee Kari Kripaa, Karo Hriday Men Vaasa. Manokaamanaa Siddh Kara, Puravahu Meree Aasa..

Jay Jay Jay Lakshmee Mahaaraanee. Sab Kee Tum Ho Shubh Kalyaanee.. Vishnu Priyaa Prakriti Bhavaanee. Saty Sanaatan Sukhadaayaanee..

Kamalaa, Kamalaasan Vaasinee. Jnyaan Vijnyaan Vilaasinee.. Sadaa Snpatti Kee Daayinee. Bhakton Kee Dukh Naashinee..

Shvet Ambar Dhaaran Karatee. Sone Kee Aabhaa Bikharatee.. Kamal Pushp Par Viraajatee. Bhakton Ke Ghar Shobhaa Laatee..

Lakshmee Jee Kee Aaratee Keeje. Dhana-dhaany Ghar Men Bhar Leeje.. Shukravaar Vrat Jo Kare Bhaaee. Lakshmee Maataa Kee Kripaa Paaee..

Jo Yah Chaaleesaa Nit Padhain. Dhan Snpatti Sukha-samriddhi Badhain.. Lakshmee Jee Kripaa Karen Taba. Snkat Mite Sukh Paae Saba..

🌸 Significance
Lakshmi Chalisa removes poverty and financial hardship, attracts wealth and prosperity, brings harmony and beauty to the home, and blesses the devotee with all comforts of life.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Lakshmi Chalisa?

Lakshmi Chalisa is a 40-verse prayer dedicated to Goddess Lakshmi, the Hindu goddess of wealth, prosperity, fortune, and beauty. It is believed to attract financial blessings and prosperity.

When to recite Lakshmi Chalisa?

Lakshmi Chalisa is most auspicious on Fridays (Shukravar) and on Diwali. It should be recited after bathing in a clean space with a lit diya before Goddess Lakshmi's image.

What are the benefits?

Regular recitation removes financial struggles, attracts wealth and abundance, brings peace and harmony at home, and grants all desires of the devoted.
