Surya Chalisa

सूर्य चालीसा

Full 40 Verses in Hindi with Meaning

📚 Chalisa 🪤 Lord Surya 🌐 Hindi + English

Surya Chalisa (सूर्य चालीसा) is a 40-verse devotional hymn to Lord Surya — the Sun God, source of all life and energy. Sunday is His day. Reciting Surya Chalisa at dawn, facing East, grants health, eyesight, and divine solar energy.

Surya Chalisa — सूर्य चालीसा

जय जय जय सूर्य भगवान। करण सहित हरि सुत बलवान॥ कश्यप पुत्र अदिति के लाला। नव ग्रह के तुम हो मतवाला॥

🌸 Significance
Lord Surya rules health, vitality, fame, and intelligence. His Chalisa removes diseases (especially eye and skin ailments), blesses with strong health, and ensures success and fame.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Surya Chalisa?

Surya Chalisa is a 40-verse prayer dedicated to Lord Surya, the Sun God. Reciting it at sunrise on Sundays is extremely auspicious for health, vitality, and overcoming eye-related ailments.

What are the benefits of Surya Chalisa?

Surya Chalisa removes diseases (especially eye and skin diseases), grants vitality and strong health, blesses with fame and success, and counteracts the malefic effects of Sun (Surya) in the horoscope.

How to recite Surya Chalisa?

Face East at sunrise, offer water (Arghya) to the Sun first, then recite Surya Chalisa 1-3 times. Sunday is most auspicious. The Surya Namaskar (12 salutations) can be done before recitation.
