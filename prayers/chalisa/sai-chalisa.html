Sai Chalisa

साई चालीसा

40 Verses to Shirdi Sai Baba in Hindi

📚 Chalisa 🪤 Sai Baba 🌐 Hindi + English

Sai Chalisa (साई चालीसा) is a 40-verse devotional hymn dedicated to Shri Sai Baba of Shirdi — the saint who transcended all religious boundaries and preached 'Sabka Malik Ek' (God is one for all). Sai Baba is revered by both Hindus and Muslims and his Chalisa is recited on Thursdays (Guruvar) at Shirdi and worldwide.

Sai Chalisa — साई चालीसा

पहले सांई के चरणों में, अर्पित करूं मैं माथ। चालीसा लिखने को, दो मुझे शक्ति साईनाथ॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

जय जय जय साईनाथ प्रभु, करहु कृपा अब धाम। भक्त जनों के संकट, लेहु प्रभु अब थाम॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

शिरडी ग्राम में तुम आए। सबको अपना दर्शन दिए॥ हिन्दू मुस्लिम सबको भाए। एकता का पाठ पढाए॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

सबका मालिक एक है, यह सत्य बताया। नाम न जात न धर्म है, बस प्रेम बताया॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

अल्लाह मालिक कह के बोले। मस्जिद में दिया जलाए॥ दिव्य दृष्टि से सब देखते। भक्तों का दुख हर पाए॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

OM साई राम साई राम साई राम। श्री साई राम साई राम साई राम॥ जो जन साई को भजते। उनके सब काम सजते॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

Pahale Saanee Ke Charanon Men, Arpit Karoon Main Maatha. Chaaleesaa Likhane Ko, Do Mujhe Shakti Saaeenaatha..

Jay Jay Jay Saaeenaath Prabhu, Karahu Kripaa Ab Dhaama. Bhakt Janon Ke Snkata, Lehu Prabhu Ab Thaama..

Shiradee Graam Men Tum Aae. Sabako Apanaa Darshan Die.. Hindoo Muslim Sabako Bhaae. Ekataa Kaa Paath Padhaae..

Sabakaa Maalik Ek Hai, Yah Saty Bataayaa. Naam N Jaat N Dharm Hai, Bas Prem Bataayaa..

Allaah Maalik Kah Ke Bole. Masjid Men Diyaa Jalaae.. Divy Drishti Se Sab Dekhate. Bhakton Kaa Dukh Har Paae..

OM Saaee Raam Saaee Raam Saaee Raama. Shree Saaee Raam Saaee Raam Saaee Raama.. Jo Jan Saaee Ko Bhajate. Unake Sab Kaam Sajate..

🌸 Significance
Sai Chalisa answers all prayers of the devotee. Sai Baba is known for fulfilling the desires of those who approach him with faith and patience. His blessings remove all suffering and guide the devotee towards God.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who was Sai Baba of Shirdi?

Shirdi Sai Baba was a spiritual master who lived in Shirdi (Maharashtra) in the 19th-20th century. He preached love, forgiveness, and unity of all religions. His famous saying was 'Sabka Malik Ek' (One God for all).

When to recite Sai Chalisa?

Sai Chalisa is most auspicious on Thursdays (Guruvar). It can also be recited any time with devotion. Lighting a diya and incense before Sai Baba's photograph makes the recitation more effective.

What are the benefits of Sai Chalisa?

Sai Baba is known to fulfill all sincere wishes of his devotees. Reciting Sai Chalisa with faith removes obstacles, heals illnesses, brings financial relief, and fulfills heartfelt desires.
