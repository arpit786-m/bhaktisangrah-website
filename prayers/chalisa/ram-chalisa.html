Ram Chalisa

राम चालीसा

Full Lyrics in Hindi with Meaning

📚 Chalisa 🪤 Lord Rama 🌐 Hindi + English

Ram Chalisa (राम चालीसा) is a 40-verse devotional hymn sung in praise of Lord Shri Ram, the seventh avatar of Lord Vishnu and the ideal king of Ayodhya. This chalisa captures Lord Ram's divine virtues, his love for Sita, his devotee Hanuman, and his victory over evil. It is recited especially on Ram Navami and Tuesdays.

Ram Chalisa — राम चालीसा

🌸 Significance
Ram Chalisa blesses the devotee with Lord Ram's divine protection, removes fear, and fills life with righteousness and truth. 'Jai Shri Ram' is the most powerful name in the universe according to Hanuman Chalisa.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ram Chalisa?

Ram Chalisa is a 40-verse devotional hymn dedicated to Lord Shri Ram, the ideal king and seventh avatar of Vishnu. It praises his divine qualities and seeks his blessings.

When to recite Ram Chalisa?

Ram Chalisa is recited on Ram Navami, Tuesdays, and Sundays. It can also be recited after reading Ramayana or during Navratri.

What are the benefits of Ram Chalisa?

Ram Chalisa provides protection from difficulties, removes fear, grants victory over enemies, and fills life with righteousness and peace.
