Bajrang Baan

बजरंग बाण

Full Lyrics in Hindi with Meaning

📚 Chalisa 🪤 Hanuman 🌐 Hindi + English

Bajrang Baan (बजरंग बाण) is one of the most powerful prayers dedicated to Lord Hanuman. It is believed to be an extremely potent prayer that provides protection from evil forces, negative energies, and all kinds of troubles. The word 'Baan' means arrow — this prayer is said to strike like an arrow, cutting through all obstacles and enemies. It is recited especially on Tuesdays and Saturdays, and during times of distress.

Bajrang Baan — बजरंग बाण

निश्चय प्रेम प्रतीति ते, विनय करैं सनमान। तेहि के कारज सकल शुभ, सिद्ध करैं हनुमान॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

जय हनुमंत संत हितकारी। सुन लीजै प्रभु अरज हमारी॥ जन के काज विलंब न कीजै। आतुर दौरि महा सुख दीजै॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

जैसे कूदि सिंधु महि पारा। सुरसा बदन पैठि विस्तारा॥ आगे जाय लंकिनी रोका। मारेहु लात गई सुर लोका॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

जाय विभीषण को सुख दीन्हा। सीता निरखि परम पद लीन्हा॥ बाग उजारि सिंधु महँ बोरा। अति आतुर यम कातर तोरा॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

अक्षय कुमार मारि संहारा। लूम लपेटि लंक को जारा॥ लाह समान लंक जरि गई। जय जय धुनि सुरपुर नभ भई॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

अब विलंब केहि कारण स्वामी। कृपा करहु उर अंतरयामी॥ जय जय लखन प्राण के दाता। आतुर होई दुख करहु निपाता॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

बाण शब्द सुनि काँपें भूता। भाग परे वे मार के दूता॥ हें हनुमत बल बुद्धि निधाना। सोइ बल अब मोहि देहु महाना॥

Nishchay prem prateeti te, vinay karain sanmaan. Tehi ke karaj sakal shubh, siddha karain Hanumaan.

Jai Hanumant sant hitkari. Sun leejai prabhu araj hamari. Jan ke kaaj vilamb na keejai. Aatur dauri maha sukh deejai.

Jaise koodi sindhu mahi para. Surasa badan paithi vistara. Aage jaay Lankini roka. Marehu laat gayi sur loka.

Jaay Vibhishan ko sukh dinha. Sita nirkhi param pad linha. Baag ujaari sindhu mahan bora. Ati aatur yam kaatar tora.

🌸 Significance
Bajrang Baan is considered one of the most powerful protective prayers in Hinduism. Regular recitation is believed to ward off evil spirits, black magic, and negative energies. It grants the devotee the strength and courage of Hanuman himself.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bajrang Baan?

Bajrang Baan is a powerful prayer to Lord Hanuman composed of verses that invoke his divine power for protection. 'Baan' means arrow, signifying that this prayer is as powerful and swift as an arrow in removing obstacles and evil.

When should Bajrang Baan be recited?

Bajrang Baan can be recited any day, but is especially auspicious on Tuesdays and Saturdays. It is recited 11, 21, or 108 times for maximum effect.

What are the benefits of Bajrang Baan?

Regular recitation of Bajrang Baan is believed to provide protection from evil spirits and negative energies, grant strength and courage, remove obstacles, and bring peace to the devotee's life.
