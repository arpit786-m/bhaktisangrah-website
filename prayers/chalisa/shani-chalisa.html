Shani Chalisa

शनि चालीसा

Full Lyrics in Hindi with Meaning

📚 Chalisa 🪤 Shani Dev 🌐 Hindi + English

Shani Chalisa (शनि चालीसा) is a devotional hymn of 40 verses dedicated to Lord Shani Dev, the ruler of the planet Saturn. Reciting Shani Chalisa, especially on Saturdays, is believed to reduce the malefic effects of Shani and bring his blessings. Lord Shani is considered the god of justice and karma in Hindu astrology.

🌸 Significance
Shani Chalisa protects from the ill effects of Sade Sati (7.5 years of Saturn transit), delays, and hardships. It teaches that Shani rewards the righteous and disciplines the wrongdoers.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Shani Chalisa?

Shani Chalisa is a 40-verse devotional hymn dedicated to Lord Shani Dev (Saturn). It praises his power, seeks his blessings, and asks for protection from Saturn's malefic effects.

When to recite Shani Chalisa?

Shani Chalisa should be recited every Saturday, ideally in the morning after bathing. It can also be recited during Shani Dasha or Sade Sati periods for relief.

Does Shani Chalisa reduce Sade Sati effects?

Yes, regular recitation of Shani Chalisa along with charitable acts on Saturdays (like donating sesame seeds, black cloth, or mustard oil) is believed to significantly reduce the hardships of Sade Sati.
