Bhairav Chalisa

भैरव चालीसा

Kal Bhairav Chalisa — Full Lyrics in Hindi

📚 Chalisa 🪤 Lord Bhairav 🌐 Hindi + English

Bhairav Chalisa (भैरव चालीसा) is a powerful 40-verse hymn dedicated to Lord Bhairav — the fierce manifestation of Lord Shiva as the master of time. Lord Bhairav is the guardian deity of Kashi (Varanasi). He rules Saturday and is the destroyer of evil, fear, and all negative forces.

Bhairav Chalisa — भैरव चालीसा

जय जय श्री काल भैरव भोले। भक्तों के दुख हरण बोले॥ काशी नगरी के कोतवाल। दंड हाथ में भयंकर काल॥

⋯ ॐ ⋯

अष्ट भैरव के तुम महाराज। सिद्ध पीठ करते हो काज॥ शनिवार को जो तुम्हें ध्यावे। सब संकट से मुक्ति पावे॥

महाकाल रूप में प्रचंड। सब दुष्टों का करते खंड॥ काशी विश्वनाथ के द्वारपाल। जय जय जय श्री काल भैरव॥

🌸 Significance
Bhairav Chalisa removes all fears, destroys enemies, protects from evil spirits and black magic, and grants Lord Bhairav's fierce protective blessings. Saturday is his most auspicious day.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Lord Bhairav?

Lord Bhairav (Kaal Bhairav) is the fierce form of Lord Shiva, manifested to destroy the ego of Lord Brahma. He is the Lord of Time, the guardian of Kashi, and the destroyer of all evil. He rides a black dog and carries a trident.

When to worship Bhairav?

Lord Bhairav is worshipped on Saturdays (his special day), on Bhairav Ashtami, and during Navratri. Kashi Bhairav temple in Varanasi is the most famous Bhairav shrine.

What are the benefits of Bhairav Chalisa?

Bhairav Chalisa provides powerful protection from evil spirits, black magic, enemies, and all fearful situations. It is especially effective for removing obstacles and granting victory.
