Santoshi Maa Chalisa

संतोषी माँ चालीसा

Full 40 Verses in Hindi — Friday Vrat Chalisa

📚 Chalisa 🪤 Santoshi Maa 🌐 Hindi + English

Santoshi Maa Chalisa (संतोषी माँ चालीसा) is a 40-verse hymn dedicated to Goddess Santoshi Maa — the goddess of contentment and satisfaction. The famous 1975 film 'Jai Santoshi Maa' made her widely popular. She is worshipped every Friday (Shukravar) with a vrat for fulfillment of wishes.

🌸 Significance
Santoshi Maa is known to be one of the most responsive goddesses — she quickly fulfills the sincere wishes of those who observe the Friday vrat. The vrat involves 16 consecutive Fridays of fasting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Santoshi Maa?

Santoshi Maa is the Hindu goddess of contentment and satisfaction, believed to be the daughter of Lord Ganesha. She became extremely popular after the 1975 Bollywood film 'Jai Santoshi Maa' depicted her miraculous powers.

How to observe Santoshi Maa Vrat?

The vrat is observed for 16 consecutive Fridays. On each Friday, fast and offer gur (jaggery) and chana (gram) to the goddess. Do not consume sour food on the vrat day.

When to recite Santoshi Maa Chalisa?

Santoshi Maa Chalisa is recited every Friday morning after bath and puja setup. It is especially powerful during the 16-Friday vrat period.
